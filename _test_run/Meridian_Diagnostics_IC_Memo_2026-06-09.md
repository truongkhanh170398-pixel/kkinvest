## I. Executive Summary

Meridian Diagnostics Holdings ("Meridian" or the "Company") is a specialty
clinical-diagnostics laboratory group operating a hub-and-spoke network across
the U.S. Southeast and Midwest. The Company runs reference labs serving
hospital systems, physician practices, and ~1,400 contracted outpatient sites,
with a growing molecular/genomics testing menu.

We propose to acquire 100% of Meridian for an enterprise value of **$920m**
(`12.0x` LTM EBITDA of ~$77m), funded with **$430m of equity** and **~$420m of
debt** (`5.5x`). The deal rationale rests on (i) durable, recurring testing
volumes, (ii) a clear margin-expansion path via lab consolidation and send-out
in-sourcing, and (iii) a fragmented regional market supporting a buy-and-build
roll-up.

hold, driven primarily by EBITDA growth (organic + tuck-ins) and deleveraging,

## V. Investment Thesis

**Why attractive (5 pillars):**
1. **Recurring, contracted volumes** — route-based diagnostics with high
   re-order rates and multi-year payer/hospital contracts.
2. **Margin expansion runway** — consolidate spoke labs, in-source high-margin
   send-out tests, automate accessioning → target +400–500 bps EBITDA margin.
3. **Fragmented market for buy-and-build** — a pipeline of sub-scale regional
   labs acquirable at `6–8x`, accretive vs. the `12x` platform multiple.
4. **Mix shift to molecular/esoteric** — faster growth, structurally higher
   margins, deepens clinical differentiation.
5. **Cash generative + deleveraging** — strong FCF conversion funds tuck-ins
   and rapid debt paydown.

**Value-creation levers:** organic growth (mid-to-high single digits), margin
expansion (ops + mix), 2–4 tuck-ins/year, and deleveraging. No multiple
expansion underwritten in base.

**100-day priorities:** finalize LIMS/billing integration plan; stand up M&A
pipeline & corp-dev cadence; launch send-out in-sourcing wave 1; payer-contract
review; management incentive plan.
